Requirements

To use this extension, your device must meet the following hardware criteria:

  • OS: Windows 10/11, macOS 13+, Linux, or ChromeOS (16389.0.0+)
  • Storage: At least 22 GB free space
  • GPU/CPU: >4GB VRAM (GPU) & >16GB RAM + 4 Cores (CPU)
  • Network: Unmetered connection (for initial model download)
